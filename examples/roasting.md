# Nimbus Coffee — Roasting Notes

## Roast Levels

We offer three roast levels. **Light** roasts are pulled just after first crack
and taste bright and fruity. **Medium** roasts are balanced with more body.
**Dark** roasts are pulled deep into second crack and taste bold and smoky.

## Origins

Our single-origin beans come from Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. The
Ethiopian beans are naturally processed and have blueberry notes. The Colombian
beans are washed and taste of caramel and citrus.

## Storage

Store beans in an airtight container away from light and heat. Whole beans stay
fresh for about 4 weeks after roasting; ground coffee is best used within a week.
Do not refrigerate or freeze opened bags, as moisture damages the beans.
