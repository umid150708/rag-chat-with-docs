# Nimbus Coffee — Customer Handbook

## Refunds and Returns

Nimbus Coffee accepts returns of unopened bags within **30 days** of purchase for
a full refund. Opened bags can be returned within 14 days if you are unhappy with
the roast; we refund 50% of the purchase price in that case. Refunds are issued to
the original payment method within 5 business days.

## Shipping

Orders over $35 ship free within the continental United States. Standard shipping
takes 3 to 5 business days. We do not currently ship internationally. Subscription
orders always ship free, regardless of order value.

## Subscriptions

A Nimbus subscription delivers freshly roasted beans every 2, 4, or 6 weeks. You
can pause or cancel any time from your account page with no fee. Subscribers get
10% off every order and free shipping.

## Loyalty Program

Members earn 1 point per dollar spent. 100 points can be redeemed for a free
12-ounce bag. Points expire 12 months after they are earned.
